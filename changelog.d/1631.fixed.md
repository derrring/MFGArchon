- **nD FDM velocity channel: usable for MAXIMIZE, and no longer silently drops a velocity** (Issue #1631, toward #1528 phase 2). `solve_fp_nd_full_system` resolved the scalar drift coefficient unconditionally at function scope, so `fp_drift_coefficient`'s (correct) fail-loud guard for non-quadratic-MINIMIZE Hamiltonians fired *before* the drift channel was consulted -- a MAXIMIZE problem could not run even when supplying `velocity_field`, the channel that exists to carry its precomputed alpha*. The coefficient is now resolved lazily, at the point of use. Separately, only `divergence_upwind` reads `interface_velocity`; the other three schemes ignored it and re-derived the drift from the zero-U dispatcher, discarding the caller's velocity (verified: velocity (2.5, -1.7) gave bit-identical output to zero). Supplying `velocity_field` with a scheme that does not consume it now raises, naming the supported schemes, instead of advecting at rate zero. `divergence_upwind` output is byte-identical. Implementing the channel in the remaining schemes is tracked in #1632.
